Note

This independent research and the Warmth Engine Observatory platform are conducted separately. The Observatory applies structured classification methodology to observable events, including the novel 7-type Coordination Connections taxonomy and 11-category industry taxonomy; this research explores theoretical frameworks through analytical observation. The Observatory's event database — its classifications, connections, and evidence documentation — is derived entirely from its published methodology and documentary evidence.

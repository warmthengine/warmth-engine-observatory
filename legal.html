Platform Description

WEO is a platform that tracks AI infrastructure coordination dynamics through verified events and the coordination connections between them. The platform provides research and analysis tools for understanding geopolitical AI infrastructure dynamics.

Use of Content

  • Free Tier: Basic event information and map visualisation are freely accessible.
  • Analyst Tier: Full analysis, framework assessments, and detailed source documentation require an active subscription.
  • Attribution: If you reference WEO data or analysis in publications or presentations, please cite "Warmth Engine Observatory" with a link to the platform.
  • No Redistribution: Analyst-tier content should not be systematically redistributed or republished without permission.

Intellectual Property

WEO's original methodological contributions — including the Coordination Connections taxonomy and the specific integration of analytical frameworks for AI infrastructure coordination — are the intellectual property of Warmth Engine.

WEO adapts established frameworks (Basel Committee G-SIB methodology, Keohane institutional cooperation theory, intelligence community verification standards) with appropriate attribution for the novel domain of AI infrastructure coordination.

The underlying event data is compiled from public sources, which retain their original ownership and licensing.

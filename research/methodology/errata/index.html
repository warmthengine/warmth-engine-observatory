WEO Methodology Errata

Warmth Engine Observatory · Methodology V1.5 · Last updated 21 July 2026 · errata.json

These are corrections to expression, not changes to methodology. Each entry aligns the published text with the methodology's own governing provision; the Manual's substantive provisions govern throughout. Both the pre-correction and post-correction states are preserved in the public archival record, and reversals — if any — are logged as new entries, never silent edits.

Qualification. An entry qualifies as an erratum only where the published text does not match the methodology's own governing provision — internal inconsistency, conversion omission, cross-reference error, or typographical error. Where a correction would require choosing between two defensible methodological readings, it is not an erratum and is routed to the Development Register.

This batch applies 17 errata (ER-001–ER-017) across the two self-hosted publications — 7 in the Manual, 10 in the Rationale — recorded in the WEO Methodology Errata Register (V1.5 publications).

About this register

Errata are applied to the self-hosted HTML publications under the WEO Errata Application Protocol and governed by the Methodology Version Management System. At the next DOI publication, applied errata are folded into the new version and this list is reset. A machine-readable mirror of the applied errata is available at errata.json.

The published DOI versions on Zenodo record the state at publication; this page and the archival record together document every correction applied since.
